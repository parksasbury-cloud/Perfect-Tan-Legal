Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 9, 2026

Perfect Tan ("we", "our", or "the app") respects your privacy. This policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and the choices you have.

Information We Collect

  • Account information. If you create an account, we collect your name and email address (or your Apple ID credentials if you use Sign in with Apple).
  • Selfie photos. If you choose to use skin-type analysis, your photo is sent securely to an AI image-analysis service (Google Gemini) to estimate your Fitzpatrick skin type. Photos are not stored on our servers and are not used to train AI models. You can skip this step entirely and choose your skin type manually.
  • Location. With your permission, we use your device location to fetch the local UV index and weather. Location coordinates are sent to weather data providers (Open-Meteo, BigDataCloud) to retrieve conditions; they are not stored by us.
  • Tanning session data. Session times, durations, and UV levels are stored on your device, and synced to our cloud database (Google Firebase) if you are signed in.
  • Purchase information. Subscriptions are processed by Apple or Google. We use RevenueCat to manage subscription status. We never see your payment details.

How We Use Information

  • To calculate personalized safe-tanning recommendations
  • To show live UV and weather conditions for your location
  • To track your tanning sessions and progress
  • To manage your subscription

What We Don't Do

  • We do not sell your personal information.
  • We do not show third-party advertising.
  • We do not store your selfie photos.
  • We do not track you across other apps or websites.

Third-Party Services

The app uses these services, each governed by its own privacy policy:

  • Google Firebase (authentication & data storage)
  • Google Gemini API (optional skin-type photo analysis)
  • RevenueCat (subscription management)
  • Open-Meteo & BigDataCloud (weather/UV and location naming; no account data shared)

Data Retention & Deletion

You can delete your account and all associated cloud data at any time from the Profile screen, or by contacting us. Local data is removed when you delete the app.

Children's Privacy

Perfect Tan is not directed at children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect data from them.

Health Disclaimer

Perfect Tan provides general guidance based on UV data and your stated skin type. It is not medical advice and cannot prevent sunburn or skin damage. Always use sunscreen and consult a dermatologist for medical concerns.
